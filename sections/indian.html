Indian Cuisine

Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with spices, herbs, and a choice of meat or vegetables, creating a flavorful Indian delicacy.

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Succulent chicken pieces simmered in a luscious tomato and butter sauce, offering a creamy and indulgent taste..

Paneer Tikka

Cubes of marinated paneer and vegetables grilled to perfection, offering a delightful vegetarian appetizer.

Chole Bhature

A North Indian favorite featuring spicy chickpea curry paired with deep-fried bread, bhature.

Tandoori Chicken

Marinated chicken cooked in a tandoor (clay oven) for smoky, charred perfection.

Rogan Josh

Aromatic Kashmiri lamb curry with a vibrant red color and a harmonious blend of spices.

Palak Paneer

Soft paneer (Indian cheese) cubes in a creamy spinach gravy, providing a wholesome and vegetarian delight.

Dhokla

Steamed and spongy gram flour cakes, seasoned with mustard seeds and curry leaves, known for their light and tangy flavor.

Masala Dosa

South Indian crispy rice crepes filled with a spiced potato mixture, often served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Vindaloo

A fiery Goan curry featuring marinated meat (usually pork or lamb) in a tangy and spicy sauce.

Malai Kofta

Deep-fried vegetable or paneer balls in a creamy tomato-based gravy, known for its decadent richness.

Aloo Paratha

Stuffed Indian flatbread with spiced mashed potatoes, typically enjoyed with yogurt and pickles.

Hyderabadi Biryani

A South Indian biryani variation, prepared with fragrant rice, succulent meat, and aromatic spices, often accompanied by raita.

Samosa

Triangular pastry filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat, popular as a savory snack or appetizer.

Tikka Masala

Tender chunks of chicken or paneer in a creamy tomato-based sauce, renowned for its rich and spiced profile.
