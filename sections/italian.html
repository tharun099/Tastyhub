Italian Cuisine

Pizza Margherita

A classic Neapolitan pizza topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil. It's a symbol of Italian cuisine worldwide.

Pasta Carbonara

Pasta Carbonara is a classic Roman dish that combines simple ingredients to create a rich and flavorful pasta dish.

Lasagna

A layered pasta dish consisting of sheets of lasagna noodles, Bolognese sauce (meat ragù), béchamel sauce, and grated Parmesan cheese. It's baked until bubbly and golden.

Risotto

Creamy rice dish cooked slowly with flavorful broth until it reaches a rich and velvety consistency. Variations include Risotto Milanese with saffron and Risotto alla Parmigiana.

Ossobuco

A Milanese specialty made from braised veal shanks cooked with white wine, broth, onions, carrots, celery, and tomatoes. It's often served with gremolata (lemon, garlic, and parsley) on top.

Bruschetta

Slices of toasted bread rubbed with garlic, drizzled with olive oil, and topped with diced tomatoes, fresh basil, and sometimes mozzarella. It's a popular appetizer.

Ravioli

Stuffed pasta parcels typically filled with a mixture of cheese, meat, or vegetables. They can be served with various sauces, such as marinara, sage butter, or creamy Alfredo.

Minestrone

A hearty vegetable soup made with a variety of seasonal vegetables, beans, and pasta, all simmered in a flavorful broth.

Caprese Salad

A simple salad from the island of Capri, featuring slices of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil leaves, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.

Pesto Pasta

Pasta (often trofie or linguine) tossed with pesto sauce, a vibrant mixture of basil, pine nuts, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil.

Fettuccine Alfredo

Flat egg noodles served with a creamy sauce made from butter and Parmesan cheese. It's a rich and indulgent pasta dish.

Cioppino

A seafood stew originating from San Francisco, typically made with a mix of fish, shrimp, clams, mussels, and crab, cooked in a tomato-based broth.

Zuppa Toscana

A hearty Tuscan soup made with Italian sausage, potatoes, kale, and sometimes white beans. It's flavorful and warming.

Torta della Nonna

A "Grandmother's cake" made with a pastry crust filled with rich custard cream and often topped with pine nuts and powdered sugar.

Cacciatora (Chicken Cacciatore)

A rustic dish made with chicken cooked in a tomato-based sauce with onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, and various herbs. It's often served with pasta or crusty bread.
