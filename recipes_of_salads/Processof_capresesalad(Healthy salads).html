Caprese Salad

A simple yet elegant Italian masterpiece featuring tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, and balsamic glaze.

Servings

4 people

Cook time

0 minutes

Prep time

15 minutes

Total time

15 minutes

the

Ingredients

  • 14 ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 21 cucumber, sliced into rounds
  • 31 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 41 green bell pepper, sliced into rings
  • 51/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 61/2 cup crumbled feta cheese.
  • 71/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 82 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 91 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 10Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 11Fresh parsley leaves for garnish

Variation

For a variation, you can use lemon juice or balsamic vinegar instead of red wine vinegar in the dressing.

step 1

Prepare the Vegetables

Wash and dry the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and green bell pepper. Cut the tomatoes into wedges. Slice the cucumber into rounds. Thinly slice the red onion.Slice the green bell pepper into rings..

step 2

Assemble the Salad

In a large salad bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, green bell pepper, and Kalamata olives. Gently toss the ingredients together to mix evenly..

step 3

Add the Feta Cheese

Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top of the salad.

step 4

Prepare the Dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and freshly ground black pepper.

step 5

Dress the Salad

Drizzle the dressing over the salad, ensuring an even distribution.

step 6

Toss and Garnish

Gently toss the salad to coat all the ingredients with the dressing.

step 7

Serve

Divide the Horiatiki Greek salad into four serving plates.

step 8

Garnish

Garnish each plate with fresh parsley leaves for a burst of color and extra flavor.
